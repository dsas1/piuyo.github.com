Organizational models for Data Science Summary

Four key components that characterize data science, organizational models

  1. The owner of the resources (the business units or a central organization)
  2. Resource allocation (how resources are deployed)
  3. Who does the allocations and makes initiatives?
  4. Who is in control (drives the agenda)?

Organizational models for Data Science New Terms or Vocabulary

Key Terms

  • Decentralized Data Science Organization: Functional areas and business units independently define and manage their data and data science initiatives.
  • Centralized Data Science Organization: manages all data and data science-related projects centrally through a group that typically includes a chief data officer.
  • Federation: The combination of decentralized and centralized management.
