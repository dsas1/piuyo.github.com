Organizational models for Data Science Easy Quiz

Organizational models for Data Science Quizzes

Which of the following represents factors that determine the organizational model most appropriate for the data science function in a business?
SOLUTION:
  • Resource allocation (how resources are deployed)
  • Who is in control (drives the agenda)?
  • The owner of the resources (business units or a central organization)
  • Who does the allocations and makes initiatives?

A business has just hired a Chief Data Officer to transition the many data science initiatives that exist across the business as part of a more coordinated strategic effort. This implies a shift from a(n) organizational data science model to a more organizational data science model.
SOLUTION: Decentralized; centralized
