15. Quizzes: Finding the Players Quizzes

Reflect

QUESTION:

We've described the "Data Science Hacker" as a role that is often hired before the other team members are in place. In your own words, how would you describe the skillset that characterizes an ideal candidate for this role?

ANSWER:

The Data Science Hacker is a generalist, experienced with many of the technologies required for digitally transformative projects. They tend to be literate in multiple specialties and can help scope out a project with an inquisitive mind and understanding of the big picture. Data Science Hackers are best able to apply and combine established packages and open-source examples to get a proof of concept in minimal time, work with the business, interact with end-users, and present results that generate excitement and momentum for data science in the business.

Reflect

QUESTION:

Hiring members of a data science team can take quite a lot of time; sometimes timelines are such that you need to find people to contribute so quickly that hiring is not possible. In your own words, please provide two strategies that you might employ to scale up a data science team in a hurry.

ANSWER:

There are many strategies that could help scale teams in a hurry. A few in particular include:

  • Hiring independent contractors rather than full-time data scientists
  • Using data science consultancies
  • Re-skilling and/or up-skilling current employees in other areas of the business who have tech or quant interests and skills
  • Partnering with university programs to offer internships and graduate fellowships
  • Engaging with providers of Machine Learning as a Service (MLaaS) solutions
