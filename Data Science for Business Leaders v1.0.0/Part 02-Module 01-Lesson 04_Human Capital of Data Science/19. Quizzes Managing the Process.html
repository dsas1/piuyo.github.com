Quizzes: Managing the Process

Which of the following is not a project management methodology that can be used to manage the data science process?
SOLUTION: Scrum

In traditional Agile methodology, software development work is managed and allocated to developers at two-week intervals called sprints. What is the primary drawback of using sprints to manage work in a data science context?
SOLUTION: Data science tasks are more speculative in nature than traditional software development tasks
