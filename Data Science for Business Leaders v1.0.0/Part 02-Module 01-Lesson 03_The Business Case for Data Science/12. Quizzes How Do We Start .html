Reflect

QUESTION:

This section introduced the concept of Rapid Prototyping. In your own words, what are two key benefits of implementing data science projects via a rapid prototyping approach?

ANSWER:

There are many advantages to rapid prototyping. Two key advantages that I have found in my work are (1) ensuring that your project and model stay close to the needs and requirements of the user; and (2) providing a way to leverage progress to generate momentum toward support and continued funding of data science in the business.
