Key Success Factors for Data Science Projects Summary

McKinsey's drivers for successful analytics in the business

  1. Aligning on Strategy
    • Obtaining a strong, unified commitment from all levels of management
    • Increasing analytics investments, with a focus on the last mile
  2. Building a foundation of data, people, and infrastructure
    • Developing a clear data strategy with strong data governance
    • Using sophisticated analytics methodologies
    • Possessing deep analytics expertise enabled by a tailored talent strategy
    • Creating cross-functional, collaborative agile teams
  3. Promoting a data-driven culture
    • Prioritizing top decision-making processes
    • Establishing clear decision-making rights and accountability
    • Empowering the front lines to make analytics-driven decisions

Key Success Factors for Data Science Projects New Terms or Vocabulary

Four scope conditions that signal readiness for data science initiatives:

  1. Data
    • Structured Data
    • Unstructured Data
  2. Infrastructure
    • Data Quality Processes
    • ETL processes
    • Data Security
  3. Business Processes
    • Machine Learning Development Lifecycle
  4. Tools and Frameworks
    • Programming languages (most commonly, Python and R)
    • Machine Learning libraries (Scikit-learn, Tensorflow, Pytorch, Keras)
    • Version control systems (Github, Bitbucket)
