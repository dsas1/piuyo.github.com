Meet Your Instructor Text

Ed Wiley, Ph.D.

Hi, I'm Ed, your instructor for this class.

I have over 20 years of experience building data science teams and leading data science initiatives across companies both large and small. I built the first Big Data team at Seagate and served as Chief Data Scientist for Sears and Kmart; I've also built teams for startups such as Nurx and Enveda Therapeutics. I often keynote and present workshops on data sciences at conferences, and have written and published a book on implementing artificial intelligence, data science, and machine learning (Artificial Intelligence: from Buzzword to Business Function.)

Prior to building my first data science team, I chaired a doctoral program in social science research methods as a faculty member at the University of Colorado, Boulder. I studied under some of the pioneers of machine learning during my doctoral studies at Stanford and also spent several years as a McKinsey management consultant in San Francisco. You can find me on LinkedIn (https://www.linkedin.com/in/edwileyphd) and Twitter (@WileyEd), where I post often about data science and machine learning.

I'm delighted to bring this course to you through Udacity. I hope you enjoy it!

Good luck!
