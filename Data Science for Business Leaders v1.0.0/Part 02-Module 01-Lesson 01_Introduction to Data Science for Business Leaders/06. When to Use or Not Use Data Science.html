When to Use or Not Use Data Science Summary

In general, data science initiatives are most appropriate when:

  • Data to support the initiative is available and accessible;
  • The initiative aligns with the strategic priorities of the organization;
  • The organization has the right human capital to carry out the initiative; and
  • The organization has the right technical architecture in place to support the initiative.

True or False: Virtually any business has the opportunity to leverage data science to improve its operations.
SOLUTION: True
