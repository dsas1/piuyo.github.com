Introduction to Data Science Summary

Data science is a field of inquiry that focuses on extracting meaningful insights from data.

For our purposes, data science will serve as an umbrella term that includes the subfields of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics.

Success in data science generally requires some combination of the following:

  • Business or domain expertise
  • Knowledge of mathematics and statistics
  • Some degree of programming skills
  • "Soft" skills such as communication and storytelling skills

The definition of Data Science

Data Science is a field of study in which we can extract meaningful insights from data through a combination of which of the following?
SOLUTION:
  • Domain expertise
  • Programming skills
  • Knowledge of mathematics and statistics
