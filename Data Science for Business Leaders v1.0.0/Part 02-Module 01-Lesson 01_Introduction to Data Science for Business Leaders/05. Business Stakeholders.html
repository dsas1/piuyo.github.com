Business Stakeholders Summary

Stakeholders for data science projects include:

  • Senior leadership (CEO or Business Unit Head)
  • Functional area representatives (e.g., Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Manufacturing)
  • Technical area representatives (Data, DevOps, Engineering)
  • Voice of the Customer

Which of the following can serve as business stakeholders for data science projects? Select all that apply
SOLUTION:
  • Business Unit heads
  • Chief Marketing Officer
  • Chief Technology Officer
  • Chief Executive Officer
  • Director of Procurement
