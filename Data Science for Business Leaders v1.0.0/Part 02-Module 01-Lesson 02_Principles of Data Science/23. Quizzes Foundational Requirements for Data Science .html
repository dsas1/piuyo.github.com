23. Quizzes: Foundational Requirements for Data Science

Reflect

QUESTION:

What are the foundational requirements for data science success? Please provide at least two examples of requirements in the space below.

ANSWER:

For data science initiatives to be successful, quite a few foundational requirements should be in place. The following are typically identified as most critical foundational requirements:

  • Intuition and deep knowledge of the problem context
  • Relevant and high-quality data
  • Versatile computational toolbox

Reflect

QUESTION:

Data scientists become most familiar with the models they end up using most often, and may develop a small stable of "go-to" models, algorithms that they tend to favor over others. While deep expertise in a given area of data science can be a strength, how might relying on a 'favorite' model or algorithm introduce problems related to the foundations of data science success?

ANSWER:

When data scientists become biased toward specific models or algorithms, they run the risk of fitting problems to their specific models, rather than letting intuition and deep knowledge of the problem context drive the choice of approach.
